I'm currently accepting new students both in the D.C.-area and virtually. My teaching style is most effective for motivated intermediate and advanced students.

 

Rate is $80/hour.

 

In broad terms, my pedagogy is centered around the development and refinement of:

- Fundamentals -- You will learn to produce sounds in an efficient, healthy manner, with even tone throughout the registers.

- The "ear" and personal standards -- You will learn to discern what needs improvement; only after a student hears what the teacher hears can they adequately improve.

- Musical rhetoric -- You will learn to effectively communicate what the composer intended, through fine attention to each detail.

- Musical character -- You will learn to develop your own unique voice while adhering to common practice guidelines.

- Cultivating belief -- You will learn substantive, practical, and effective methods of mental training, guidance on audition preparation, and how to build trust in oneself.

- Practice techniques -- You will learn the most effective, science-based methods to plan, structure, and engage in personal practice.
