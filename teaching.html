performing teaching contact

 

I'm currently accepting new students both in the D.C.-area and virtually. My teaching style is most effective for motivated intermediate and advanced students.

 

  • Fundamentals — produce sounds in an efficient, healthy manner, with even tone throughout the registers.

  • Standards — understand the standards for excellence and discern areas that require improvement.

  • Rhetoric — effectively communicate composers' intentions through fine attention to detail.

  • Character — develop a unique musical voice while adhering to common performance practice

  • Belief — cultivate trust in oneself and one's abilities through practical methods of mental training and performance psychology principles.

  • Practice — learn the most effective, science-based methods of how to plan, structure, and engage in personal practice.
